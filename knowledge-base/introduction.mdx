## What is TryPost?

TryPost is an open-source social media scheduling platform. It lets you create, schedule, and publish posts across multiple social networks from a single dashboard.

## Key features

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Multi-platform posting" icon="share-nodes">
    Publish to LinkedIn, X (Twitter), Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, Threads, Pinterest, Bluesky, and Mastodon from one place.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Scheduling" icon="calendar">
    Schedule posts in advance and let TryPost publish them automatically at the right time.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Workspaces" icon="building">
    Organize your social accounts by brand or client with separate workspaces.
  </Card>
  <Card title="API & MCP" icon="code">
    Automate your workflow with the REST API or connect AI assistants via MCP.
  </Card>
</Columns>

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a workspace">
    Sign up and create a workspace for your brand or client.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your accounts">
    Link your social media accounts (LinkedIn, X, Instagram, etc.) to your workspace.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create and schedule posts">
    Write your content, customize it per platform, and schedule it for the best time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish automatically">
    TryPost publishes your posts at the scheduled time across all selected platforms.
  </Step>
</Steps>
