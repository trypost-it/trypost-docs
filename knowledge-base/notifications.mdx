## What are notifications?

TryPost sends notifications to keep you informed about important events — like when a post is published, when a social account disconnects, or when a team member joins your workspace.

## Notification types

| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Post published** | A scheduled post was successfully published to all platforms |
| **Post failed** | A post failed to publish to one or more platforms |
| **Post partially published** | A post was published to some platforms but failed on others |
| **Account disconnected** | A connected social account lost its authorization |
| **Invite received** | You received an invitation to join a workspace |
| **Member joined** | A new member accepted an invitation and joined your workspace |
| **Member removed** | A member was removed from your workspace |

## Notification channels

You can receive notifications through two channels:

| Channel | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **In-app** | Notifications appear in the notification bell inside TryPost |
| **Email** | Notifications are sent to your email address |

## Notification preferences

Go to **Settings > Notifications** to control which notifications you receive and through which channels. You can enable or disable each notification type independently for each channel.

<Tip>
  At minimum, keep **Post failed** and **Account disconnected** notifications enabled so you can quickly react to issues.
</Tip>

## Email configuration

Email notifications require a working mail configuration. TryPost uses [SendKit](https://sendkit.dev?utm_source=trypost&utm_medium=docs&utm_campaign=notifications) as the default mailer with a free plan of 3,000 emails/month — more than enough for notification emails. See the [Configuration guide](/self-hosting/configuration) for setup details.
