## What are social accounts?

Social accounts are the connections between TryPost and your social media profiles. When you connect an account, TryPost can create and publish posts on your behalf.

## Supported platforms

| Platform | Account types |
|----------|--------------|
| **LinkedIn** | Personal profiles and company pages |
| **X (Twitter)** | Personal accounts |
| **Facebook** | Pages |
| **Instagram** | Business and creator accounts |
| **TikTok** | Personal accounts |
| **YouTube** | Channels |
| **Threads** | Personal accounts |
| **Pinterest** | Business accounts |
| **Bluesky** | Personal accounts |
| **Mastodon** | Accounts on any instance |

## Connecting an account

Go to **Accounts** in the dashboard and click the platform you want to connect. You'll be redirected to the platform's authorization page where you grant TryPost permission to post on your behalf.

<Note>
  Each platform has different requirements and scopes. See the individual [platform guides](/platforms/overview) for details.
</Note>

## Active vs. inactive accounts

Each social account can be toggled between **active** and **inactive** states:

- **Active** — The account is included when creating new posts. Platform entries are created for all active accounts.
- **Inactive** — The account is excluded from new posts. Existing scheduled posts are not affected.

This is useful when you want to temporarily pause posting to a specific account without disconnecting it.

## Account status

| Status | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Connected** | The connection is working and TryPost can publish posts |
| **Token expired** | The authorization token has expired and needs to be reconnected |
| **Disconnected** | The account has been disconnected |

## Via the API

You can list social accounts and toggle their active state via the [API](/api-reference/endpoint/list-social-accounts) or through an AI assistant connected via [MCP](/ai/introduction).

```bash
curl -X GET https://app.trypost.it/api/social-accounts \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer tp_your_api_key"
```
