## Team roles

Each workspace member has a role that determines their permissions:

| Role | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Owner** | Full access to the workspace. Can manage billing, delete the workspace, and manage all members. Each workspace has exactly one owner. |
| **Admin** | Can manage posts, social accounts, hashtags, labels, and invite or remove members. Cannot delete the workspace or manage billing. |
| **Member** | Can create and manage posts, use hashtag groups, and apply labels. Cannot manage social accounts, invites, or workspace settings. |

## Inviting members

Go to **Settings > Team** and click **Invite Member**. Enter the email address and select a role for the new member.

The invited person receives an email with a link to join your workspace. If they don't have a TryPost account, they'll be prompted to create one first.

<Note>
  Pending invitations can be revoked at any time before they're accepted.
</Note>

## Removing members

Workspace owners and admins can remove members from the workspace. Go to **Settings > Team**, find the member, and click **Remove**.

Removing a member:
- Revokes their access to the workspace immediately
- Does not delete any posts they created
- Does not affect other workspaces they belong to

## Multiple workspaces

A single user can belong to multiple workspaces with different roles in each. Use the workspace switcher in the sidebar to navigate between them.
