# Instagram

TryPost supports posting to Instagram Business and Creator accounts.

<Note>
  Personal Instagram accounts are not supported. You need a **Business** or **Creator** account connected to a Facebook page.
</Note>

## Connect your account

1. Go to **Accounts** in the TryPost dashboard
2. Click **Connect Instagram**
3. Authorize TryPost through Facebook
4. Select the Instagram account to connect

## Supported content types

| Type | Aspect ratio | Media | Description |
|------|-------------|-------|-------------|
| **Feed** | 1:1 | Up to 10 images or 1 video | Main feed post |
| **Reel** | 9:16 | 1 video (required) | Short-form vertical video |
| **Story** | 9:16 | 1 image or video (required) | Temporary content (24 hours) |

## Self-hosting setup

<Accordion title="Configure Instagram API credentials">
  1. Go to [Meta for Developers](https://developers.facebook.com/) and create an app
  2. Add the **Instagram** product
  3. Set the redirect URL to `{APP_URL}/accounts/instagram/callback`
  4. Request permissions: `instagram_basic`, `instagram_content_publish`
  5. Add to your `.env`:

  ```env
  INSTAGRAM_CLIENT_ID=your_app_id
  INSTAGRAM_CLIENT_SECRET=your_app_secret
  INSTAGRAM_CLIENT_REDIRECT="${APP_URL}/accounts/instagram/callback"
  ```
</Accordion>
